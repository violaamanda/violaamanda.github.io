My Story

A story of my experiences in Japan!

I went to Japan around 2 years ago. I went there with my family. We were there for a week. My memories about the trip might be a little blurry, but I still remember a few places that I plan to visit again someday. The places are Shinjuku, Disney Land, Disney Sea, and Universal Studio. Shinjuku is a good place for shopping and the others are good for having fun. Another thing I didn't get to do last trip is hunting for halal food, so I wish someday I will be able to do that!
